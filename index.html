About.Me

I am now on version 3.0 of my life.

Version 1.0

Version 1.0 was a very creative time in my life. During that time, I performed as a professional stand-up comedian. I landed a gig as an independent joke writer for the Tonight Show with Jay Leno. I played the piano as an emcee at the Comedy Store and also was a regular there too! Those were incredibly fun and creative times in my life.

Version 2.0

Version 2.0 was another incredible time in my life. It was a productive time. I got married. I built several successful businesses. I practiced law during that time. I traveled extensively. I became an endurance athlete.

Version 3.0

Version 3.0 is now! I am combining my humor, creativity and productivity using web and software development as my career of choice.

I am a full stack web developer. Is that even a real thing? Bottom line is that I am a web developer. Learning new technologies makes me tick. I tinker with technology. I build with technology. Is there any greater technological development tool than the keyboard,? The keyboard gives me creative and logical expression.

With that in mind, on this site you can view my portfolio, my interests, and learn a little bit more about me, including relevant trivia. Enjoy your day!
